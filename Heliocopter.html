HELIOCOPTER

Delivering Value Dense Payloads To Locations In Need


Designers:
- David Hernandez
- Jackie Bai
- Lucas Dondertman
- Tony Liu
- Tristan Gervais

Our Mission:
Thousands of remote villages around the globe lack the transportation or infrastructure to receive the delivery of goods in a suitable time frame. 

Our solution is an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle that can economically deliver high value density payloads across long distances while also requiring minimal infrastructure.  This UAV will allow organizations to quickly send goods long distances without the need for pilots, runways or special training. 

Design Features

Mechanical

  • Twin Thrust Vectoring Motors
  • Carbon Fiber Clam Shell Construction
  • Single Operation Latch
  • Isolated Electronics Enclosure
  • Hinge on Leading Edge to Acces Payload from Rear
  • Wing Span of ~2m

Electrical

  • 200 W Solar Cell Array
  • BMS With Battery Cell Balancing
  • Maximum Power Point Tracking Solar Charging Loop
  • Redundant IMU
  • Custom 6S4P Battery Pack Using 18650 Cells

Software

  • Separate Hover and Fixed Wing Flight Modes
  • Closed Loop Roll/Pitch/Yaw Stabilization
  • Closed Loop Altitude Control
  • Ground Station Software to Plan Missions
  • GPS Way Point Tracking

Specifications

  • 100 Km Range @ 150 KPH
  • 400 Km Range @ 60 KPH
  • Unlimited Range With Landing for Solar Recharge
  • 2 Kg Max Payload
  • Insulated Payload For Temperature Sensitive Goods
  • Two 40x80 cm Cargo Bays
  • Autonomous Take Off, Cruising, and Landing
  • Self-Charging Using Solar Panel
  • Wireless Mission Programming
